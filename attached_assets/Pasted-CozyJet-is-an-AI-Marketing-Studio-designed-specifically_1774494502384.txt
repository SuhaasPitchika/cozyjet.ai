This comprehensive SaaS platform solves the universal problem of social media content creation by transforming your completed work, ideas, and trending topics into platform-optimized, viral-ready content across LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, and Reddit in under 30 seconds. Unlike traditional productivity trackers or surveillance tools, CozyJet operates as an active content generation workspace where users maintain complete control—simply describe what you built (via voice input, screenshot upload, or direct integration with GitHub, Figma, and Notion), and our three specialized AI agents—Content Brain (Skippy), Content Planner (Snooks), and Content Factory (Meta)—immediately spring into action to create professional marketing content. Built on a robust FastAPI backend with PostgreSQL database, Redis caching, Celery background processing, and powered by state-of-the-art language models including Anthropic Claude Sonnet 4, Google Gemini Pro, and OpenAI GPT-4, CozyJet delivers enterprise-grade reliability with startup-level agility. The platform features intelligent content calendar management, trend monitoring that identifies viral opportunities in real-time, brand voice learning that adapts to your unique style with every edit, multi-platform batch generation that creates content for all social channels simultaneously, and comprehensive analytics showing exactly what content drives engagement—making it the definitive AI marketing solution for technical creators who want to build their personal brand without the marketing headache.
Revolutionary Three-Agent AI System: Content Brain, Content Planner, and Content Factory
CozyJet's proprietary three-agent architecture represents a paradigm shift in AI-powered marketing automation, with each agent serving a distinct role in the content creation pipeline while working seamlessly together to deliver exceptional results. Content Brain (Skippy) serves as your intelligent content input system, offering five versatile methods to capture your work: voice input where you simply speak what you built and AI enhances it into marketing-ready material, screenshot upload that analyzes images of your GitHub commits, Figma designs, or code to extract meaningful insights, direct integration synchronization with popular tools like GitHub (analyzing commits, pull requests, code changes), Figma (design projects, iterations, collaborations), and Notion (documentation, product updates, roadmaps), manual entry with AI-powered enhancement that transforms brief descriptions into compelling narratives, and quick-start templates for common scenarios like "I shipped a feature," "I wrote code," or "I designed UI" that intelligently fill in details through contextual questions. Content Planner (Snooks) functions as your AI content strategist and scheduling assistant, maintaining a smart content calendar that visualizes upcoming posts across all platforms, identifies content gaps with suggestions like "You haven't posted on Twitter in 3 days—here's an idea," predicts optimal posting times based on audience engagement patterns, creates 30-day content series plans that maintain narrative consistency, monitors trending topics in your niche and suggests timely content angles, provides platform-specific posting strategies (LinkedIn 3x/week for case studies, Twitter daily for threads, Instagram 2x/week for visual content), and enables content recycling by identifying high-performing past posts worth republishing with fresh perspectives. Content Factory (Meta) operates as your full-service AI copywriter with five distinct creation modes: work-based content generation from completed projects, idea-based creation for thought leadership pieces even without recent work, trend-based content that capitalizes on viral topics, repurposing existing content (converting blog posts into tweet threads, video transcripts into LinkedIn articles), and engagement mode that drafts intelligent replies and comments to build your social presence strategically—all while learning your unique brand voice through continuous analysis of your edits and preferences.
Comprehensive Backend Architecture: FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis, Celery, and LangChain Integration
The CozyJet backend represents production-grade engineering excellence, built on a modern Python stack optimized for performance, scalability, and developer experience. At its core runs FastAPI 0.109+, a high-performance web framework chosen for its native async/await support that enables non-blocking database queries and external API calls, automatic OpenAPI documentation generation at /docs for seamless frontend integration, Pydantic-based request/response validation that catches errors before they reach production code, and processing speeds comparable to Node.js and Go making it one of the fastest Python frameworks available. Data persistence leverages PostgreSQL 15+ with a carefully designed schema including users table (UUID primary keys, bcrypt password hashing with 12 rounds, subscription tier management for free/pro/studio/agency plans, JSON-based voice profiles and PII blocklists, email verification workflows), content_seeds table (storing raw work data from voice input, screenshots, integrations with flexible source_data JSON field, tagging system for categorization and search), content table (managing generated posts through their complete lifecycle from draft to scheduled to published with platform-specific optimization, engagement tracking, and error handling), calendar_entries table (scheduling system with AI-powered engagement predictions and status workflows), trends table (hourly-updated trending topics with relevance scores and related keywords), post_analytics table (comprehensive engagement metrics including views, likes, comments, shares, and calculated engagement rates), and content_templates table (user-created reusable formats with performance scoring based on historical success). Redis 7+ serves dual purposes as both a caching layer (storing frequently accessed data like user profiles with 1-hour TTL, generated content with 1-hour cache to avoid redundant AI calls, productivity scores with 10-minute TTL, and analytics with 5-minute refresh) and message broker for Celery 5.3+ background task processing that handles scheduled content publishing via platform APIs (LinkedIn ugcPosts, Twitter v2 tweets, Instagram Graph API), trend detection running hourly to identify viral opportunities, engagement analytics collection from social platforms, voice profile automatic updates based on user edits, and content suggestion generation combining work history with trending topics. The AI orchestration layer utilizes LangChain 0.1+ to manage three specialized agents: Content Brain powered by Claude Sonnet 4 or GPT-4 Vision for intelligent work analysis and enhancement, Content Planner using Gemini Pro for content calendar optimization and trend analysis, and Content Factory driven by Claude Sonnet 4 with per-user conversation memory for maintaining context across refinements—all integrated through clean abstraction layers that enable model switching, prompt template customization, chain composition for complex workflows, and automatic retry logic with exponential backoff for API resilience.
Developer-Focused Features: API Architecture, WebSocket Real-Time Communication, and Deployment Pipeline
CozyJet's technical architecture prioritizes developer experience through comprehensive API design, real-time features, and streamlined deployment workflows. The REST API exposes 30+ endpoints organized into logical routers: /api/auth handles user authentication including signup with email verification, login returning JWT access tokens (24-hour expiry) and refresh tokens (7-day expiry), token refresh mechanisms, email confirmation workflows, and logout with token blacklisting; /api/skippy manages content input with voice-input processing, screenshot-upload analysis, integration-sync for GitHub/Figma/Notion, content-seeds CRUD operations, and enhancement pipelines; /api/snooks provides content planning with calendar retrieval, suggest-content based on gaps and trends, trends monitoring, and optimal-times calculation using engagement data; /api/meta powers content generation with generate-from-idea for thought leadership, generate-from-trend for viral opportunities, repurpose for content transformation, batch-generate for multi-platform creation, reply-suggestions for engagement, and analyze-voice for automatic brand voice detection; /api/analytics delivers performance insights, competitor monitoring, and actionable recommendations; and /api/templates enables content template management with performance-based ranking. WebSocket endpoints at /ws/notifications provide real-time updates when content publishes successfully, new trends are detected, scheduled posts go live, engagement milestones are reached, and team members approve content—all without polling, using persistent connections that automatically reconnect on network interruption and support message queuing for offline reliability. The deployment pipeline leverages Docker containerization with docker-compose.yml defining five services (postgres for data persistence, redis for caching and job queuing, backend running FastAPI via Gunicorn with 4 UvicornWorker processes for optimal performance, celery_worker for background task execution with automatic retry and error handling, and celery_beat for scheduled job triggering) enabling local development via docker-compose up -d for instant environment setup, production deployment to Railway through GitHub integration with automatic Dockerfile detection, managed PostgreSQL and Redis provisioning with connection strings auto-injected as environment variables, zero-downtime deployments using health checks at /health endpoint, automatic SSL certificate provisioning for HTTPS, horizontal scaling support for handling traffic spikes, and built-in monitoring with logs, metrics, and alerting—allowing developers to ship from local to production in under 15 minutes with enterprise-grade reliability, security through JWT authentication and rate limiting (100 requests/minute authenticated, 5/minute auth endpoints, 10/minute AI generation), CORS protection with origin whitelisting, bcrypt password hashing, and Pydantic input validation that prevents SQL injection, XSS attacks, and malformed requests.
